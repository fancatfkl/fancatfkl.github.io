My work as Open Educational Resources (OER) Commissioner at Students' Society of McGill University (SSMU) involves mapping students' needs for free textbooks, creating free and openly-licensed textbooks and fostering an open community at the SSMU and beyond.

SSMU OER
============

All our resources are stored on the Open Science Framework (OSF). They are also included in the catelogue of QPIRG's Free Textbook Loan Program with digital copies (and, soon, physical copies available for loan).
